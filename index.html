My Professional Goals

My goal is to be a website designer one day very soon. Right now, I am attending Dallas College to learn the skills I will need to be a successful website designer. This semester alone I am learning about JavaScript, Java, and Photoshop!

My long term goals are to work for myself and create websites for clients freelance. I would love the ability to work with small to medium size businesses and even individuals to create the websites of their dreams! I would also love the freedom of creating my own schedule and having that flexibility to create a desirable work/life balance.

Most of all, I just want to create a career doing something that I love doing - designing websites!
